Victoria Sharks Clinch the Varsity 7s Championship!

In a thrilling display of skill and determination, the Victoria Sharks emerged victorious in the 2024 Varsity 7s Championship, solidifying their reputation as the top team in the league.

The final match, held at the prestigious Kyambogo University Stadium, was a nail-biting affair that kept fans on the edge of their seats. The Sharks led by their captain Tago showcased their prowess with a well-coordinated offense and a resilient defense.

Their relentless drive and team spirit culminated in a decisive 3-1 victory over their rivals, Team X. The win marks a significant milestone in the club's history, reflecting months of hard work, strategic planning, and unwavering dedication from every player and staff member.
